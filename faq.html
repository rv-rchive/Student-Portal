Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

How do I update my profile?

Go to the Profile page and click the upload or edit buttons beside your information. You can also change your display picture from there.

Where can I see my registered subjects?

You can view all your currently enrolled subjects from the Subjects tab in the sidebar.

How can I download my registration form?

Your ORF (Official Registration Form) is available in the Accounts & Payments page under the Account Documents section.

Requirements & Clearance

How do I upload my requirements?

Go to the Requirements page and click the Upload button under each document card.

Why is my clearance still pending?

Clearance can only be finalized after in-person verification by each department. Please visit the concerned office physically to complete the process.

Account / Technical

Can I switch to dark mode?

Yes! Click the moon icon in the top-right corner or enable it through Settings → Theme.

I found a bug. Where can I report it?

You can report portal issues under the Feedback page by selecting “Issue/Problem” as your feedback type.
